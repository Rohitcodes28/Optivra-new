Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Optivra.

What services does Optivra offer?

We offer AI & Machine Learning consulting, Data Science & Analytics, Full-Stack Development, Cloud Automation, and Technology Strategy. Each solution is tailored to your specific business needs.

How do you ensure data security?

We follow industry best practices including GDPR compliance, end-to-end encryption, regular security audits, and strict access controls. Your data never leaves secure environments without your explicit consent.

What industries do you work with?

We work across FinTech, Healthcare, E-commerce, Manufacturing, and Education. Our team has deep domain expertise in each industry, ensuring solutions that are contextually relevant.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope. A simple AI integration might take 4-6 weeks, while enterprise-scale transformations can span 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during consultation.

Do you offer ongoing support after project completion?

Absolutely! We offer flexible maintenance and support packages including bug fixes, performance monitoring, security updates, and feature enhancements as your business grows.

What makes Optivra different from other tech consultancies?

We combine deep technical expertise with business acumen. Our team includes former Fortune 500 executives, PhDs in AI/ML, and seasoned engineers focused on delivering measurable business impact.

How do you handle project pricing?

We offer flexible pricing models: fixed-price for well-defined projects, time & material for evolving requirements, and dedicated teams for long-term partnerships. Transparent with no hidden fees.

Can you work with our existing tech stack?

Yes! We're technology-agnostic and work with your existing stack whether it's Python, Java, JavaScript, AWS/Azure/GCP, or legacy systems. We integrate seamlessly with your current workflows.
